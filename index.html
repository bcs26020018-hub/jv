Study Resources & Tips

Tools and advice to help you succeed.

📚

Time Blocking

Block 2–3 hour chunks for deep work. Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 min focus, 5 min break.

✍️

Note-Taking

Use Cornell or outline method. Summarise in your own words and link ideas to previous topics.

🔄

Spaced Repetition

Review material after 1 day, 3 days, then weekly. Use flashcards (e.g. Anki) for facts.

🛌

Rest & Sleep

7–8 hours sleep improves memory. Short naps (20 min) can boost afternoon focus.
